About SolFinder

Research-Driven Innovation, Real-World Solutions

Solfinder Research Pvt Ltd is an IT and research-oriented company based in Lalpur, Ranchi, Jharkhand. Incorporated on 5 March 2019 as a One Person Company (OPC), it is registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Government of India.

The company specializes in software development, technology consulting, research and development, electronics, embedded systems, and IT-enabled services. With a strong focus on innovation and quality, Solfinder Research develops reliable technology solutions that help businesses and institutions embrace digital transformation and solve real-world challenges.

Our Mission

To develop innovative software, research-driven solutions, and digital technologies that help businesses solve real-world problems through quality, reliability, and continuous innovation.

Our Vision

To become a leading technology and research company by delivering intelligent software solutions, promoting innovation, and contributing to India's digital transformation.

Our Values

Innovation, Integrity, Quality, Customer Focus, Collaboration, and Continuous Learning guide every project we undertake and every solution we deliver.
